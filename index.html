Royalties distribution

25% back to MONI$ holders

25% of the royalties generated from the sales and mints of our NFTs, starting March 1st, are distributed back to our NFT holders every month based on the number of days they own the NFT. This incentivizes holding onto the NFTs and rewards early adopters, creating a more engaged and loyal community. We want to create a sustainable revenue stream to support the development of the MONI$ Game while giving back to the community that believes in the project. Join the MONI$ community today and start earning rewards for your NFT ownership!

1 NFT = 1 point

If you hold at least 5 MONI$ *, each one represents one point

Points x days

For every day you hold a MONI$ NFT you'll receive 1 point for each

Royalties / points

The royalties are distributed by the numbers of points

July 2023 June 2023 May 2023 April 2023 March 2023

Example: You held 5 MONI$ for 30 days, which gave you a total of 150 points. If the total number of points attributed to other holders is 1000 and the amount of royalties to be distributed is 500 XRP, the holder from the example would receive 65~ XRP (500/1150 = 0,4347826087 XRP per point, 0,4347826087 x 150= 65,217391305 XRP)

The distribution always takes place in the following month and will continue until the Gem Tokens are launched. From that time onwards, MONI$ holders who stack their MONI$ NFTs will receive tokens as a reward.

*A minimum of 5 MONI$ is required to be eligible to enter the royalties distribution.
