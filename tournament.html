Rules

  • Eligibility: To participate in the tournament, users must have a Twitter account and be at least 18 years old.
  • Participation: To qualify to win a prize user must follow @MONIS_NFT on Twitter, vote, retweet and tag at least one friend.
  • Voting: Users can vote for their favorite MONI$. Only one vote per user is allowed.
  • Voting period: The voting period will last for 24h.
  • Minimum votes required:

    A minimum of 10 valid votes in each fight (following, voting, retweet, tag 1 friend) is necessary to move to the Quarter-Finals.

    A minimum of 15 valid votes in each fight (following, voting, retweet, tag 1 friend) is necessary to move to the Semi-Finals.

    A minimum of 20 valid votes in each fight (following, voting, retweet, tag 1 friend) is necessary to move to the Final.

    A minimum of 25 valid votes in the final fight (following, voting, retweet, tag 1 friend) is necessary to determine a winner.

    If there aren't enough valid votes, a new fight with the same conditions will be held the next day. All other fights will get pushed one day.

  • Winner:After the voting poll closed, we'll randomly select a follower that retweted the fight post and tagged at least one friend. If we are unable to contact the winner to collect the prize, a new user will be drawn.
  • Ties: In the event of a tie, a tie-breaking vote will be held.
  • Cheating: Any attempt to manipulate the voting process, such as creating fake accounts or asking for votes through means other than Twitter, will result in disqualification.
  • Prizes: The winner of each round of the tournament will receive a prize, which is stated in this website.
  • Release: By participating in the tournament, users agree to release the organizers from any liability.
  • Changes: The tournament organizers reserve the right to make changes to the rules, prizes and other details as necessary without previous warning.
