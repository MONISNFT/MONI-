Earn Crystals and trade them

As you adventure through the game, you'll have the chance to earn Crystals. These aren't just shiny objects for Moni and his companion firefly to accumulate. They serve as valuable assets that can be traded in the Dwarven Mines for power-ups, cosmetic upgrades, and even sell to other players or buy it from them.

Power-Ups & Alchemy

As you progress through the game, you'll come across a variety of power-ups. Each one offers Moni a unique ability to help him on his journey. These valuable power-ups can be traded at the Dwarven Mines for crystals.

And if you're facing a tough boss, unlock the secrets of Alchemy by merging two power-ups together to create an entirely new one with even more exciting abilities.

Fizzy, your (tiny) companion

Moni will be accompanied by a small and friendly firefly throughout his journey: Fizzy.

Fizzy will act as a guide and warn Moni of any dangers ahead, keep track of his health and even catch otherwise inaccessible crystals.

As Moni explores the mysteries of Alchemy, he'll be able to improve Fizzy's abilities and make them an even more helpful companion!

A gift from the Dwarves

Unlock the secrets of the Dwarven Mines with the MONI$ holders exclusive magical pickaxe!

This powerful artifact automatically mines crystals, depositing them directly into your inventory.

The rarest your MONI$ are, the faster the mining speed, making it the ultimate tool for collecting crystals.

  1. Are MONI$ NFTs related to the game?

    We plan to integrate the collection with the game to give exclusive content for MONI$ holders.

  2. What is the current status or phase of development for the MONI$ game?

    The game is in pre-production phase.

    The pre-production phase of game development is a crucial stage in which the various ideas and concepts for the game are fleshed out and brought to life. It is a time of collaboration and brainstorming that's critical to determine the scope and direction of the game.

    What's completed:

    • Develop the game's narrative and characters.
    • Determine the technology constraints of the game and how they will impact gameplay and content.
    • Design and create concept art for the game's visual elements.
    • Plan out the mechanics and physics of the game.
    • Determine the "fun factor" of the game.
    • Create a project schedule and assign tasks to team members.
    • Identify any potential challenges or obstacles that may arise during development.
