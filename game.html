MONI$ Mobile Game

MONI$ Mobile Game (provisory title) is a turn-based puzzle-adventure game where the player must guide Moni through challenging courses filled with traps and hazards. Along the way, you must avoid dangers and collect treasures.

The game will be released on iOS and Android late 2023 (date subject to change).

We bet you have a lot of questions

Here are some answers...

  1. Are MONI$ NFTs related to the game?

    Short answer: no, and yes.

    Although the MONI$ NFTs are not a promise of a game and act as stand-alone collectibles, profits coming from the collection will make it possible to fund the development of the game.

    We plan to integrate the collection with the game to give exclusive content for MONI$ holders.

  2. What kind of exclusive content there will be for MONI$ holders?

    There's a lot on the table that we need to test first. Some ideas are:

    • Exclusive power-ups/ lives for NFT holders based on the rarity percentage
    • Using the NFT in the game as a skin
    • Royalties back to holders
    • MONI$ token
  3. Will it be a play to earn type of game?

    We are in a very early stage of development, so at this point, there are no specific details we can share about a play-to-earn reward system.

  4. On which platforms will MONI$ game be launched?

    In the first phase, we plan to develop the game for Apple devices (iOS, iPad OS and tvOS) and Android devices.

    After that, we'll start working on the ports of the game for the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X, Xbox Series S and Nintendo Switch.

  5. When will the game be launched?

    Late 2023

    Provisory date. Can be changed without notice.
